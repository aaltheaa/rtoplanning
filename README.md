# Office Attendance

Track your office attendance with a simple calendar UI.

## What it does

- **Calendar view**: See the month at a glance and quickly understand which days count as “in office”.
- **Mark status per day**: Track each day as **in-office** or **OOF** (out of office).
- **Counts & summaries**: Automatically totals your in-office days for the selected month and helps you track progress toward your RTO goal.
- **Choose your tracking algorithm**: Pick the compliance rule/algorithm you want to use to evaluate attendance.
- **Set a start date**: Define when your tracking period begins so weekly and rolling compliance windows line up with your policy.
- **Weekly compliance**: See whether each week is compliant, and understand how it rolls up.
- **Rolling 12-week view**: Review compliance across the associated **12-week period** for any given week.
- **Shareable export link**: Export your settings + attendance history into a link you can bookmark/save, then **import back into the site** later to restore everything.
- **Lightweight & local-first**: Designed to be fast and easy to use without a complicated setup.
